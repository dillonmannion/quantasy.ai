# Welcome to Quantasy Alpha!

Thank you for being part of the Quantasy alpha testing team! Your feedback is critical to helping us build the most transparent and useful fantasy football tools.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1.  **Access the App**: Go to [quantasy.fly.dev](https://quantasy.fly.dev) (or your provided local URL).
2.  **Sign In**: Use your email to sign in via the magic link.
3.  **Connect League**: Navigate to the **Connect** page and enter your Sleeper username to import your leagues.
4.  **Explore**: Once connected, you can access all features from the dashboard.

## 🛠️ Features to Test

### 1. Draft Assistant (`/draft`)
*   **Rankings**: Verify that the VBD-based rankings make sense for your league's scoring.
*   **Filters & Search**: Try filtering by position and searching for specific players.
*   **"Show Your Work"**: Expand the player details to see exactly how their VBD score was calculated.
*   **Mock Draft**: Start a mock draft, pick players, and ensure recommendations update correctly.

### 2. Roster Optimizer (`/roster`)
*   **Lineup Analysis**: View your current roster and see if the suggested starters align with your intuition.
*   **Projections**: Check the projected points for your starters and bench.

### 3. Trade Calculator (`/trade`)
*   **Build Trades**: Add players to the "Give" and "Receive" zones.
*   **Fairness Meter**: Watch the meter update in real-time as you add/remove players.
*   **Trade Logic**: Read the "Show Your Work" explanation for why a trade is considered fair or unfair.

### 4. Waiver Recommendations (`/waivers`)
*   **Top Pickups**: See who the algorithm suggests you add from the waiver wire.
*   **Value Analysis**: Understand the VBD impact of potential waiver additions.

## 📱 Mobile Testing
Quantasy is designed to be mobile-first. Please try these features on your phone:
*   **Swipe Actions**: In the Draft Assistant, try swiping to mark players as drafted.
*   **Layout**: Ensure all buttons and text are readable on smaller screens.

## ⚠️ Known Issues & Limitations
*   **Monte Carlo Simulation**: Advanced draft simulations are deferred to v1.1. Rankings are currently based on static VBD.
*   **Sleeper Only**: Only Sleeper leagues are supported for the alpha.
*   **Projections**: Projection data is updated every 24 hours.
