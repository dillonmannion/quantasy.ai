## Why Quantasy is Different

### "Show Your Work" Transparency
Every recommendation comes with a clear explanation of the underlying data and logic. No black boxes. We believe you should understand *why* a player is valued a certain way.

### League-Specific Personalization
Unlike generic rankings, Quantasy calculates VBD based on YOUR league's exact scoring settings. Research shows personalized projections outperform generic rankings by **+4.7 points/week**.

### Multi-Source Dynasty Values
We integrate **FantasyCalc**, **KeepTradeCut**, and **DynastyProcess** values in one place. KTC alone has over 23M crowdsourced data points, and we combine that with market data to give you the most accurate picture.

### Sleeper-Native Integration
Direct API integration means no sync issues, no Chrome extensions, no manual data entry. It's fast, secure, and reliable.

### Mobile-First Design
Built for mobile from day one with smooth animations and intuitive navigation. Inspired by the visual "juice" of games like Balatro.

## Privacy & Analytics

Quantasy uses **Posthog** for anonymous feature usage tracking and **Sentry** for error monitoring. We respect Do Not Track headers and never collect personally identifiable information (PII). Our analytics help us understand which features are most valuable and where users get stuck.
